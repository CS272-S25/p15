CS/LIS 472 Grading Overview

In CS/LIS 472 (Intro to Web Development), grading is divided into two parts: group projects and common assignments, totaling 100 points.

Key Components:

Project Overview (25 pts):
Project Points Details
CMS Project 10 pts Group project completed in the first half of the semester. Graduate students must submit a 2-page reflection (graded as acceptable/unacceptable).
HTML/CSS/JS Project 15 pts Group project completed in the second half of the semester. Graduate students must submit a 2-page reflection (graded as acceptable/unacceptable).
Common Grading Breakdown (75 pts):
Component Points Details
Intro Survey 1 pt Quick survey at the start of the course.
Weekly Discussions 10 pts 12 discussions, 2 lowest scores dropped.
Weekly Assignments 34 pts 11 assignments, 3 pts each + HW0 worth 1 pt.
Attendance 5 pts 24 classes, 7 dropped, 0.25 pts each.
Quizzes 25 pts 6 quizzes, 5 pts each, 1 quiz dropped.
